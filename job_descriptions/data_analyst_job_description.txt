Data Analyst Position - Advanced Analytics Professional

We are looking for a skilled Data Analyst to join our analytics team and drive data-driven decision making across the organization. The successful candidate will have strong technical skills and the ability to translate complex data into actionable business insights.

Key Responsibilities:
- Analyze large datasets to identify trends, patterns, and business opportunities
- Develop and maintain automated reports and dashboards using SQL, Python, and BI tools
- Perform statistical analysis and predictive modeling to support business decisions
- Clean, validate, and prepare data for analysis from multiple sources
- Create compelling data visualizations and presentations for stakeholders
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define metrics and KPIs
- Conduct A/B testing and experimental design for product optimization
- Support data governance initiatives and maintain data quality standards

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Economics, or related field
- 3+ years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence
- Advanced proficiency in SQL for data extraction and manipulation
- Strong programming skills in Python or R for statistical analysis
- Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, or similar)
- Knowledge of statistical methods and machine learning concepts
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving abilities
- Strong communication skills to present findings to non-technical audiences

Preferred Qualifications:
- Master's degree in Data Science, Statistics, or related quantitative field
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) and big data technologies
- Knowledge of machine learning frameworks (scikit-learn, pandas, numpy)
- Experience with version control systems (Git) and collaborative development
- Familiarity with database management and data warehousing concepts
- Experience in agile development environments
- Industry certifications in data analysis or business intelligence
- Experience with advanced analytics techniques (time series, clustering, NLP)

Technical Skills:
- Programming: Python, R, SQL, Excel (advanced)
- Databases: PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB, Snowflake
- Visualization: Tableau, Power BI, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly
- Tools: Jupyter, Git, Docker, Airflow
- Cloud: AWS S3, Redshift, Azure, Google BigQuery
- Statistics: Hypothesis testing, regression analysis, experimental design

This role offers the opportunity to work with cutting-edge analytics technologies and make a significant impact on business strategy through data-driven insights.
