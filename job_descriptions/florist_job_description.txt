Florist Position - Creative Floral Designer

We are seeking a passionate and creative Florist to join our boutique flower shop. The ideal candidate will have a strong artistic eye and experience in floral design and arrangement.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design and create beautiful floral arrangements for weddings, events, and daily orders
- Maintain fresh flower inventory and ensure proper care and storage
- Consult with customers on floral needs for special occasions
- Create seasonal displays and window arrangements
- Process flower deliveries and maintain shop cleanliness
- Handle cash register and customer transactions
- Provide exceptional customer service and floral advice

Required Qualifications:
- High school diploma or equivalent
- 2+ years of floral design experience preferred
- Strong artistic and creative abilities
- Knowledge of flower types, care, and seasonal availability
- Excellent customer service and communication skills
- Ability to work flexible hours including weekends and holidays
- Physical ability to lift flower arrangements and stand for extended periods

Preferred Qualifications:
- Formal floral design training or certification
- Experience with wedding and event planning
- Knowledge of plant care and horticulture
- Experience with retail sales and inventory management
- Familiarity with POS systems
- Social media and photography skills for showcasing work

Work Environment:
- Retail flower shop setting
- Standing for long periods
- Working with water, chemicals, and sharp tools
- Cooler environments for flower storage
- Fast-paced during peak seasons (Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, etc.)

This position offers the opportunity to work in a creative environment, bringing joy to customers through beautiful floral designs for life's special moments.
